Want to deposit pennies?

If you have physical pennies and want to mint CENT tokens against them, you'll need to get them to our vault. This form starts that process.

The Pennies

No minimum. No maximum (though we might need to talk logistics if you're showing up with a truckload).

About You

We'll use this to publicly verify your deposit, which is good for everyone's trust.

Proof of Pennies

Show us what you've got. Include a piece of paper with your X handle visible in the frame so we know it's really you. JPG, PNG, or PDF up to 25MB.

One More Thing

Post something about CENT (be creative), tag @stablecentmoney, and paste the link here. Think of it as your application essay, but funnier.

What happens next?

  • 1 We verify your photo and review your submission
  • 2 If approved, we'll reach out to coordinate delivery logistics
  • 3 Your pennies arrive at the vault and get counted
  • 4 We mint CENT tokens to your Solana wallet
  • 4 You join the crowds of proud CENT holders

A note on rare pennies

Got wheat pennies, steel pennies, or anything unusual? Let us know in your tweet. We have a separate program for rare and collectible coins that might be more interesting than standard deposits.

Yes, we actually want you to deliver the pennies. No, we're not joking. This is genuinely how this works.
